# 🔥 DESIGNER AGGRESSIVE FONTS + LARGER TEXT

## New Ultra-Aggressive Font Stack

### Headings: Teko
- **Style:** Extremely condensed, ultra-bold, designer aggressive
- **Weights:** 300-700 (using 600-700 for maximum impact)
- **Character:** Sharp, angular, military-inspired
- **Perfect for:** Massive headlines that demand attention

### Body: Rajdhani
- **Style:** Bold, geometric, strong, modern
- **Weights:** 300-700 (using 600-700 for readability with impact)
- **Character:** Technical, futuristic, powerful
- **Perfect for:** Body text that still feels aggressive

## Font Size Increases

### Subheadings (Hero, Sections)
- **Before:** text-xl to text-2xl (20-24px)
- **After:** text-2xl to text-4xl (24-36px)
- **Weight:** font-semibold (600)

### Paragraphs
- **Before:** text-lg (18px)
- **After:** text-xl to text-2xl (20-24px)
- **Weight:** font-semibold (600)

### Features/Lists
- **Before:** text-sm to text-base (14-16px)
- **After:** text-base to text-lg (16-20px)
- **Weight:** font-semibold (600)

### Credentials
- **Before:** text-sm (14px)
- **After:** text-base to text-lg (16-20px)
- **Weight:** font-semibold (600)

## Why Teko + Rajdhani?

**Teko:**
- Used by extreme sports and tactical brands
- Ultra-condensed = maximum aggression
- Sharp angles = military precision
- Designer-quality aggressive aesthetic

**Rajdhani:**
- Geometric = technical, modern
- Bold weights = strong presence
- Highly readable even at larger sizes
- Pairs perfectly with Teko's aggression

## Typography Hierarchy

```
Hero Headline:     text-9xl (128px) - TRAIN HARD. RISE TOGETHER.
Section Headings:  text-8xl (96px)  - ELITE TRAINING
Page Titles:       text-6xl (60px)  - ALEX AGUILAR
Card Titles:       text-4xl (36px)  - SELECTION PREP
Body Text:         text-lg (18px)   - font-light
Labels:            text-xs (12px)   - tracking-[0.4em]
```

## Copy Style Guide

### Headlines
- Short. Powerful. Direct.
- Maximum 5 words per line
- Use periods for emphasis

### Body Copy
- Short sentences
- One idea per sentence
- Active voice
- No fluff

### CTAs
- Action verbs
- 2-3 words max
- Examples: "Start Training", "Get Started", "Transform Now"
